Engagement Models

Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) Model

Our FTE pricing model provides dedicated, highly skilled pricing professionals who operate as an extension of your commercial team. These experts deliver end-to-end support across bid pricing, cost modeling, margin optimization, and strategic pricing initiatives.

By embedding FTE resources within your organization, TRI-K-TEC enables predictable costs, knowledge retention, and operational continuity—ideal for enterprises seeking sustained pricing excellence and long-term strategic advantage.

Job-to-Job (Project-Based) Model

Our Job-to-Job pricing model delivers specialized, on-demand expertise for targeted pricing initiatives. TRI-K-TEC supports one-time bids, market assessments, price optimization projects, and short-term commercial engagements.

This flexible model offers rapid execution, cost efficiency, and access to niche pricing skills—enabling high-impact results without long-term commitments.
